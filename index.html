About Me

Hey!

My name is Vikram. I am a front-end web developer specializing in JavaScript and React. I have a master's degree with a focus on User Experience, from Breda University of Applied Sciences. My professional background also involves Quality Assurance, and Game Development, specifically as a game and level designer.

Through a combination of my game design, UX, and web development skills, I design and create responsive and user focused interfaces for a seamless and pleasant user experience which also benefits the company.

I live in a small city called Breda in the south of the Netherlands where my free time is occupied by either bouldering, researching and making presentations about the topics I am currently interested in or tinkering with game engines, when I am not playing dungeons & dragons with friends.
